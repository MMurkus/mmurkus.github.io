GAM-2252: Rap Off

I worked with a team of three to create a card game for our second project in this class. The idea behind it is similar to how Cards Against Humanity is played. Players draw three cards and must make a rap out of them within two minutes, then all of the players not rapping must vote on who had the best rap in the round. I collaborated on coming up with some of the cards, but mostly worked on writing the rules of the game, printing the cards, and physically putting them together. I also suggested the idea of creating a cardbord boombox to hold everything that was required to play.
