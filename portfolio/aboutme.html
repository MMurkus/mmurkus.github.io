About Me

I've been in North Carolina for a majority of my life and have grown up alongside video games throughout my childhood, so much so that I knew I wanted to be involved in the creation process. Once my undergraduate journey at High Point University began, I Computer Science tutor and Sound Crew Captain of the Marching Panthers, which has sharpened my problem solving and interpersonal communication skills. Outside of classes and school, I spend my time walking, working on projects, playing/researching games, and volunteering.
